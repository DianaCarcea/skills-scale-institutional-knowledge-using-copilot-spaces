# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA/Testing Lead

### Role Summary
QA and Testing Leads design and execute test strategies to ensure features meet acceptance criteria and maintain high quality standards. They work with developers and product managers to define testability requirements early.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategy and approach for each feature
- Create and maintain test cases and acceptance criteria validation
- Execute manual and automated testing
- Identify and document defects with clear reproduction steps
- Work with developers to resolve quality issues before release
- Plan and execute smoke tests and regression testing
- Provide quality metrics and risk assessments

### Goals
- Ensure features are production-ready and meet acceptance criteria
- Reduce escaped defects to production
- Enable confident, low-risk releases

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning and acceptance criteria refinement
- Daily standups to flag blockers and test progress
- Quality reports and release sign-offs

---

## Stakeholder / Sponsor

### Role Summary
Stakeholders and Sponsors represent business needs, approval authority, and success visibility. They provide resources, remove impediments, and ensure projects remain aligned with business strategy.

### Responsibilities
- Define business objectives and success metrics
- Approve project charter and major decisions
- Provide budget and resource allocation
- Escalate and resolve external blockers
- Review progress and provide feedback
- Communicate outcomes to leadership and other stakeholders

### Goals
- Ensure project delivers business value
- Remove organizational and resource barriers
- Maintain executive visibility and alignment

### Typical Communication
- Monthly stakeholder update reviews
- Milestone reviews and approvals
- Escalation pathways for critical blockers
- Release announcements and impact reporting

---

## Technical Lead / Architect

### Role Summary
Technical Leads and Architects provide technical strategy, design oversight, and engineering excellence guidance. They ensure solutions are scalable, maintainable, and aligned with long-term technical vision.

### Responsibilities
- Lead technical design and architecture decisions
- Review design documents and implementation approaches
- Identify and mitigate technical risks
- Establish coding standards and best practices
- Mentor developers and conduct technical reviews
- Support performance, security, and scalability planning
- Act as escalation point for complex technical issues

### Goals
- Enable sustainable, high-quality technical delivery
- Reduce technical debt and future rework
- Build team capability and knowledge

### Typical Communication
- Technical design reviews and architecture discussions
- Sprint planning for sizing and risk assessment
- Code reviews and technical mentoring
- Risk register updates for technical dependencies

---

## Scrum Master / Iteration Lead

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters and Iteration Leads facilitate team processes, remove blockers, and coach the team on agile practices. They focus on process health and enabling the team to execute effectively.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, standups, and retrospectives
- Remove impediments and escalate blockers
- Protect team focus and manage meeting efficiency
- Coach team on agile practices and continuous improvement
- Track velocity and help refine estimation
- Manage sprint board and workflow
- Support knowledge sharing and team learning

### Goals
- Enable team productivity and predictability
- Foster psychological safety and continuous improvement
- Maintain process discipline and transparency

### Typical Communication
- Facilitation of all sprint ceremonies
- Daily sync on blockers and progress
- Retrospective action item tracking
- Metrics and velocity trends
